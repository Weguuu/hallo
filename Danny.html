Hi, Bebii.

It was three months ago on this very day when we started talking, that day when I first saw you, first time we exchanged words, first time our skins touched, and your hand around my arm. I can vividly remember every passing second of that moment, for it was the day you saved the dying belief of a hopeless romantic in the idea of genuine love—reigniting the kindlings of hope that struggled to stay alive.

I know this is too much or far too over the top to make when we’re still in our dating phase, but I really just want to show how much I appreciate you as you, Daniel. No words can express nor numbers can quantify the value of your presence and the sheer luck of me finding someone as hardworking, determined, smart, intelligent, skilled, adorable, attractive, and most importantly, as genuine as you.

I taught myself to detach to people I’m unexclusively with, but no matter how hard I try, I just can’t bring myself to stop and distance my heart from this. I guess it is true when they say “The heart wants what the heart wants”. In all of my dating experience, Danny, I’ve never met someone like you, the very first instance where I can fully trust someone I’m still dating.

You are worth every single ounce of my time, efforts, and the entirety of my being—giving it my all to make you my partner, subsequently, yours. Ensuring you that I will be here in every step of the way and in every page of your book.
In this and every life, I choose us all the time.
